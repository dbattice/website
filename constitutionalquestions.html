Expand the House

Fix the Senate to be truly representative of the states and to express states' power against the rest of the federal government

Proposal to make the Senate a Bundesrat: chosen by state lower house majority. Minimum 2 senators. Changes due to population take effect when the House sits after redistricting. Additional senators at for 1,2, 5 and every 10 million. All senators from the same state must vote the same way. Senate can only suspend legislation including budget and tax bills. Treaties still require senate approval as do constitutional amendments (confirmed by voting public). Also federal impeachments. Every vote is majority. Only absolute majority for constitutional and presidential votes (for choosing president instead of electing president). Basically France, Czechia, or Austria powers, with Germany inspired members and procedures. Can also do reports, investigations, and introduce legislation. Not much of an argument to keep the Senate wildly disproportional while its members are directly elected. Have a constitutional amendment clarifying federal relations.

Also, planting oak trees for your grandkids or that you will never sit in the shade of. George Monbiot has written or talked about politics of the future too. All of this is running counter to democracies being increasingly dominated by old people, and this is more true of voters. George Monbiot too.
